How to play: Use your arrow keys (or swipe on touch devices) to move the tiles.
Tiles slide as far as possible.
If two tiles representing consecutive Fibonacci numbers collide (1+1, 1+2, 2+3, 3+5, etc.), they merge into the next Fibonacci number (2, 3, 5, 8, etc.)!
Other combinations (like 2+2 or 1+3) will not merge.
